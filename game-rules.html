If don't know how the game works this is a right Left click Before Starting about how the game works I would appreciate that you gave your precious time to this So , Thank You .

*First of all you need to do is have your speaker at it's max output or full sound else you can put your earphones on . *

The working of game is very simple , firstly , you would be given a sound and a blink at one of the tiles , then , you have to tap that tile , then , you would be given another blink on another tile . Now , a very important step , you have heard the tile you have to tap the tile which you heard first and then you have to tap next tile that you heard .

Basically What I'm trying to say is that you need to remember the blinking of the tiles and then tap accordingly . This is same as memory game that we all ave played in which a sequence of words are said and new words are added and again player says the sequence with a new word added .
